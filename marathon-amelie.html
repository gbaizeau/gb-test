Training & Race Tips üí°

+
  • Nutrition: Practice your race day fueling strategy on long runs. Don't try anything new on race day! Aim for 30-60g of carbs per hour.
  • Hydration: Drink water consistently throughout the day, especially before, during, and after runs. Consider electrolytes for long runs.
  • Rest & Recovery: Sleep is crucial for muscle repair and energy. Aim for 7-9 hours. Listen to your body; take extra rest if needed.
  • Pacing: Start your marathon slightly slower than your goal pace. Don't get carried away by the race day excitement!
  • Race Day Gear: Wear comfortable, tested shoes and clothing. Nothing new! Prepare for different weather conditions.
  • Mental Game: Visualize success. Break the race into smaller, manageable segments. Stay positive!
